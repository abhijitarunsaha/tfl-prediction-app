🏆 TFL FIFA World Cup 2026 Rules

Welcome to the TFL Prediction League! Please read the scoring rules carefully before submitting your predictions.

📅 Submission Rules

  • Predictions for each match must be submitted at least 15 minutes before kick-off.
  • Once submitted, predictions cannot be modified.
  • Tournament predictions remain open only until their respective deadlines.

⚽ Match Prediction Rules

1️⃣ Match Result

Correct Match Result +20
Incorrect Match Result −10

2️⃣ Exact Score

Exact Score Prediction +20
Correct Goal Difference +10

✔ Exact Score takes precedence. Goal Difference points are not awarded if the Exact Score has already been predicted correctly.

3️⃣ Goalscorers

Correct Goalscorer +10 each
Predicted player scores an Own Goal −10

Number of predicted scorers for each team cannot exceed the number of goals predicted for that team.

4️⃣ Over Prediction Penalty

Predicted total goals exceed actual goals by 3–4 −10
Predicted total goals exceed actual goals by 5 or more −20

No penalty applies if the actual total goals scored exceed the predicted total goals.
